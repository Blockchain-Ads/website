HOW IT ALL BEGAN

The problem was familiar to many: wanting to run crypto ads on top-tier networks but repeatedly getting rejected. Frustration grew as even specialized crypto advertising networks failed to yield results. Vlad and Jack, two seasoned AdTech engineers, knew this struggle all too well. Driven by innovation and a clear understanding of the gaping void in the Web3 advertising landscape, they decided it was time to take matters into their own hands. Thus, Blockchain-Ads.com was founded in January 2022. Through relentless effort and a commitment to authenticity, they built a platform that revolutionized crypto advertising, bringing real solutions to real crypto traffic. Connecting wallet addresses with advertising IDs for pinpoint targeting, Vlad and Jack created a tool tailored specifically for crypto project founders and web3 marketing companies, setting a new standard in the industry.
